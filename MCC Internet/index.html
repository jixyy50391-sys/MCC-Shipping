About Our Company

MCC Shipping is currently chartering and operating three foreign-flagged vessels from MC Group Limited, with the first vessel delivered on 10 October 2025. As part of its strategic expansion plan, the Company will progressively charter an additional seven foreign-flagged vessels by the end of 2026, all under long-term charter arrangements.

In line with MCC Group’s long-term operational strategy and subject to market demand, MCC Shipping intends to further expand its fleet with six additional vessels.

Committed to environmental sustainability, all vessels in the fleet comply with and exceed IMO Tier III emission standards and meet EEDI Phase III requirements. The vessels are methanol dual-fuel ready, supporting the global shipping industry’s transition toward greener and more sustainable operations.

Environmental & Technology

Low Carbon Emissions

Our eco-friendly ships use advanced low-sulfur fuel systems and methanol dual-fuel engines, cutting SOx, NOx, and CO₂ emissions by 60–70% compared to conventional vessels, fully meeting the latest IMO environmental standards.

Energy Efficiency

Equipped with exhaust gas treatment systems (scrubbers), shaft generators, vortex dampers, duct shrouds, and special low-resistance hull coatings, these ships reduce energy consumption by 15%.

Intelligent Management & Marine Protection

Latest smart navigation systems optimize routes and monitor performance. Advanced ballast water treatment and non-toxic antifouling paints minimize marine pollution and protect ecosystems.
